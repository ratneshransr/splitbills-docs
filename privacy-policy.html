Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

SplitBills ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SplitBills mobile application ("App").

By using SplitBills, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Data Purpose
Phone number Account creation and authentication via OTP
Name Display name within the app
Email address (optional) Account recovery and communication
Expense data Tracking and splitting expenses with groups
Group information Managing shared expenses with other users

Information Collected Automatically

Data Purpose
Device information App compatibility and crash reporting
Push notification token Sending notifications about expenses and settlements
Analytics data Improving app performance and user experience

Contacts (with your permission)

If you grant permission, SplitBills can access your device contacts to help you add members to groups. Contact data is used only for this purpose and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate you via phone number and OTP verification
  • Enable expense tracking, splitting, and settlement features
  • Send push notifications about group activity, expenses, and settlements
  • Display your name and profile to other group members
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Token-based API access

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers, including:

  • Profile information (name, phone number, email)
  • Personal expenses
  • Group memberships
  • Notification preferences and settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Control notification preferences
  • Revoke contacts permission through your device settings

To delete your account, go to Profile > Delete Account in the app, or visit our Account Deletion page.

7. Children's Privacy

SplitBills is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Notification Permissions

SplitBills may request permission to send push notifications. You can customize which types of notifications you receive within the app's Notification Settings, or disable them entirely through your device settings. Notification types include:

  • Group invitations
  • New and updated expenses
  • Deleted expenses
  • Group settlements
  • Payment requests
  • Payment confirmations

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
