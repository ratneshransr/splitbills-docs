Account & Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can delete your SplitBills account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the SplitBills app on your device
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Option 2: Request via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include the phone number associated with your account.

Send a deletion request to:

splitbillshelp@gmail.com

What Data Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Profile information Deleted
Phone number Deleted
Email address Deleted
Personal expenses Deleted
Group memberships Deleted
Notification preferences Deleted
Currency settings Deleted

Processing Time

  • Email requests: Processed within 3 business days
